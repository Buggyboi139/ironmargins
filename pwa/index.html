How to Install IronMargins

What is a PWA? IronMargins is built as a Progressive Web App (PWA). This means you don't need to visit an App Store to download it. Instead, you install it directly from your web browser. Once installed, it behaves exactly like a native app on your home screen, complete with offline capabilities and full-screen formatting.

Browser Requirements

While browsers like Firefox or DuckDuckGo are great for everyday browsing, they often block the ability to save Progressive Web Apps properly. For IronMargins to format perfectly as a native app on your mobile device, please follow the platform-specific instructions below.

Step-by-Step Installation Guide

For iOS (iPhone/iPad)

  1. Open Safari and navigate to IronMargins.com.
  2. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the screen (the square with an arrow pointing up).
  3. Scroll down the menu and select Add to Home Screen.
  4. Confirm the prompt. IronMargins will now appear on your device's home screen alongside your other apps.

For Android

  1. Open the Google Chrome app and navigate to IronMargins.com.
  2. Tap the browser menu icon (usually three vertical dots in the corner).
  3. Scroll down and select Add to Home screen (or "Install app").
  4. Confirm the prompt. IronMargins will now appear on your device's home screen.
